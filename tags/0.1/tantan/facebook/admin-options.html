Facebook Plugin for WordPress

This plugin will retrieve your Facebook photos and allow you to easily post them to your WordPress powered blog.

Installation and Usage: Just follow the onscreen prompts to link this plugin to your Facebook account. To insert photos into your posts, just click the Photos tab in the edit screen and then click on a thumbnail.
